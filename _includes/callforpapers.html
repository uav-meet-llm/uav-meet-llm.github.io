Call for Papers

The workshop will include a call for papers. The papers will be presented in poster session, and selected papers will also be oral presented as highlighted contributions.

Topics of Interest

  • LLM-Driven Autonomous Navigation
  • Platforms, Datasets or Benchmarks relative to UAVs
  • Large language model as a task planner for UAVs
  • Large language model as a motion planner/monitor for UAV control
  • Multimodal large language model for UAV perception tasks
